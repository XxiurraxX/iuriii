Nike X OFF-White

Cult-status fashion label and brainchild of designer Virgil Abloh, Off-White shook sneaker culture with groundbreaking collaborations with Jordan, Converse and Nike. Paying homage to the history of each shoe while pushing design boundaries, Off-White silhouettes such as the Air Jordan 1, Converse All-Star and Nike Air Force 1 bridge the gap between sportswear and high fashion.
